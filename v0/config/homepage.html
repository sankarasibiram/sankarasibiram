श्रीगुरुभ्यो नमः

Welcome to Sankarasibiram
अज्ञोऽप्यश्रुतशास्त्राण्याशु किल व्याकरोति यत्कृपया।
निखिलकलाधिपमनिशं तमहं प्रणमामि शङ्कराचार्यम्॥

Sri Sankaracarya, out of his abundant grace, to propagate the correct understanding of the vedas and the vedanta, wrote commentaries, prakarana granthas, and stotras to help sadhakas of varying interests, intellect, and spiritual development.

It now befalls on us to:

  • Study Sri Sankaracarya's teachings for self development.
  • Understand the philosophy we are born into.
  • Kindle interest in the next generation to learn and propagate the works.
Learn a sloka a day from Sri Sankaracarya's works by choosing a grantha that suits your interest and intellect.

Additional resources

There are many resources on the internet to learn the works of Sri Adi Sankaracarya. While choosing the source pay attention to the following:
  • Pravachana/Book
    • If the pravachanakartha/author belongs to a guru parampara
    • If the pravachanakartha/author explains the meanings taught by traditional gurus and sastras
  • Singers
    • If the pronuciation is proper (pay attention to mahapranas and samyukaksharas)
    • If the padavibhajana is correct
    • If the bhava of the text is rendered correctly with bhakti
