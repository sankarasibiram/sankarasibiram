श्रीगुरुभ्यो नमः

Welcome to शङ्करशिबिरम्
अज्ञोऽप्यश्रुतशास्त्राण्याशु किल व्याकरोति यत्कृपया।
निखिलकलाधिपमनिशं तमहं प्रणमामि शङ्कराचार्यम्॥

श्री शङ्कराचार्य, out of his abundant grace, to propagate the correct understanding of the वेदs and the वेदान्त, wrote commentaries, प्रकरण ग्रन्थाs, and स्तोत्राs to help साधकाs of varying interests, intellect, and spiritual development.

It now befalls on us to:

  • Understand the philosophy we are born into.
  • Study श्री शङ्कराचार्य's teachings for spiritual development.
  • Kindle interest in the next generation to learn and propagate the works.
Learn a श्लोक a day from श्री शङ्कराचार्य's works by choosing a ग्रन्थ that suits your interest and intellect.

Additional resources

There are many resources on the internet to learn the works of श्री शङ्कराचार्य. While choosing the source pay attention to the following:

  • प्रवचन/Book
    • If the प्रवचनकर्ता/author belongs to a गुरुपरम्पर
    • If the प्रवचनकर्ता/author explains the meanings taught by traditional गुरुs and शास्त्रs.
  • Singers
    • If the pronunciation is proper (pay attention to महाप्राणs and संयुक्ताक्षरs)
    • If the पदविभजन is correct
    • If the भाव of the text is rendered correctly with भक्ति
