प्रश्नोत्तररत्नमालिका परिचयः

Prasnottararatnamalika is one of the many works of Sri Adi Sankaracarya. It consists of 67 slokas and 182 questions and answers. In this text, sishya poses a question and the guru answers it. Answers are very cryptic, often just one word. A variety of topics are covered.
