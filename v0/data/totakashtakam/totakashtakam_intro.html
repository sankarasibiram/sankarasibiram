तोटकाष्टकम् परिचयः

श्री शङ्कराचार्य had a शिष्य named गिरि. He was not learned like the other शिष्यs. However, he was ever attentive and studious in serving the आचार्य.

Once, other शिष्यs became impatient as आचार्य was waiting for गिरि to start the day's lessons. Observing this, आचार्य bestowed all the knowledge of the शास्त्रs to गिरि instantaneously. On entering the class, गिरि praised his गुरु, श्री शङ्कराचार्य, in eight verses composed in तोटक, a difficult छन्दस्. This स्तोत्र is called तोटकाष्टकम्. Observing the beauty and eloquence of the work, गिरि was henceforth called तोटकाचार्य.

Even today, it is customary to read or sing तोटकाष्टकम् and do साष्टाङ्गनमस्कारम् after every verse to श्री शङ्कराचार्य.
